Types of Facilities

Patient Rooms

Our facility has 50 fully furnished patient rooms, each equipped with modern amenities and an ensuite bathroom to ensure the comfort and privacy of our residents.

Recreational Rooms

There are three recreational rooms available for various activities, including a Game Room, Social Hall, and Arts & Crafts Room, providing residents with diverse leisure options.

Staff Rooms

We offer 10 staff rooms, including break rooms and office spaces, to support our dedicated team members in their day-to-day operations.

Medical Rooms

The facility includes five medical rooms, such as Therapy Rooms and Medical Examination Rooms, where residents can receive treatments and consultations from healthcare professionals.
